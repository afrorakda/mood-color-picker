# Mood Color Picker A non-verbal mirror for your mind. Extract your current emotional state through the simple act of choosing a color. ## 🚀 Concept: Emotion Without Words In a world overflowing with information and the pressure to articulate everything, we often lose touch with how we truly feel. Mood Color Picker bypasses the complexity of language. By dragging your finger across a spectrum and releasing it on the color that "feels right," you gain an immediate, intuitive snapshot of your internal state. ## ✨ Features - **Tactile Exploration:** Drag your finger across the full-screen gradient to explore the nuances of color. The final decision happens the moment you release your touch. - **Psychological Insight:** Based on color psychology, each selection is mapped to a "Mood Keyword" and a brief analytical description to help you categorize your current energy. - **Immersive Result:** The entire interface transforms into your chosen color, allowing you to "soak" in the mood without distracting UI elements. - **Dynamic Contrast:** Smart logic automatically adjusts text color (black or white) based on the brightness of your selection, ensuring perfect legibility every time. - **One-Tap Reset:** Tap anywhere on the result screen to instantly return to the picker. No hunting for small buttons. ## 🛠 How to Use 1. **Explore:** Drag your finger (or mouse) over the color canvas. 2. **Decide:** Release when you find the color that resonates with your current mood. 3. **Reflect:** Read the keyword and description. Take a moment to acknowledge that feeling. 4. **Repeat:** Tap the screen to reset and find a new color whenever your mood shifts. ## 📐 The Philosophy This tool is a "digital detox" for your emotions. It follows the "1 Tool / 1 Action" rule by focusing solely on the transition from visual intuition to self-awareness. It doesn't track you or save your data—it only exists to serve your present moment. --- **No login / No tracking / No ads / Browser only** Developed by afrorakda © 2026
